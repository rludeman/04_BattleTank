Tank Game
Game Design Document
Author: Ryan Ludeman
Date: 7/24/2019
----------------------------

Contents
 - Concept
 - Rules
 - Requirements
 
 
Concept
----------------------------
Game about controlling a virtual tank and destroying the enemy tank before it destroys you.


Rules
----------------------------
YOu win when you succesfully destroy the enemy tank.
You lose when your tank is destroyed.  
The player can control the tank in a realistic manor (treads, turret, cannon) in order to manuevor and aim at destroying the enemy.
The player cannot leave the arena or manipulate the tank in an unrealistic way.


Requirements
----------------------------
Tank assets
	- Turret with barrel and eatures
	- tank body with armor and features
	- tank tracks that maneuver like a tank
	
Sounds
	- tank noises (engine, heavy metalic noises, cannon noises, rotating noises, explosions)
	- Projectile noises (whistling, explosinons)
	- terrain noises (ground shifting, moving, debris falling)

Effects
	- projectile trail
	- barrel flare
	- esplosion effects (fire, ground, shockwave)
